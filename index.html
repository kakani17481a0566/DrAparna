Vision & Mission

My vision is to revolutionize Early Childhood Education and Care (ECEC) in India by empowering teachers and children with the world's best learning methodologies.

My mission is to bring high-quality education and care to the grassroots level, ensuring every child receives an opportunity to learn, grow, and thrive. Through RITZY Group of Companies and My School Italy, I strive to bridge the gap between research, psychology, and practical early learning.

Milestones

First Milestone – UK Queen’s Award

Awarded the prestigious UK Queen’s Award for Excellence in Studies — the only non-British recipient in the last 27 years of Britain’s educational history.

2004 – EU’s Joint Research Commission Award

Honored as Best IT Consultant of the Year by the EU’s Joint Research Commission for dedicated work in the Human Medicines Department, Ispra, Italy.

From a successful IT career, I transitioned back to academia — completing a Master’s in English from Cambridge and a Doctorate in Child Psychology from the University of Essex.

This phase marked a deep personal evolution. As a woman entrepreneur, vulnerability often manifested as uncertainty, risk, and emotional exposure. Embracing these emotions instead of resisting them gave me the courage to grow and lead with empathy and resilience.

2014 – Open Knowledge Award

Received the World’s most prestigious Open Knowledge Award for Best Business Initiative. This was a defining turning point in my life and entrepreneurial journey.

The same year, I launched My School ITALY, becoming the first Italian preschool franchise in India — fulfilling my mother’s dream of bringing one of Europe’s best early education systems to millions of children in India.

2019 – Guinness World Record

Recognized by the Guinness Book of World Records as the Most Humanitarian Person of the World for impactful work in war-torn African regions and with tribal communities across Southeast Asia.

June 2019 – European Business Awards

My School ITALY was chosen among millions of European industries as a "Ones to Watch" Company by the European Business Awards for excellence in teaching methodologies.

The innovations — DECANT (a teaching tool) and MILESTONES (an assessment system) — reflect over 30 years of experience in ECEC and deep child psychology research.

In 2014, Temple University (USA) accepted my research introducing Neuro-Linguistic Programming (NLP) into ECEC, making My School ITALY the first preschool in the world to embed NLP into its curriculum.

June 2019 – India Didactic Award

Ritzy Schools received the India Didactic Award (IDA) for its innovative curriculum and methodologies.

December 4, 2019 – National Winner of Italy

My School ITALY was declared National Winner of Italy by the European Business Awards.

December 14, 2019 – MSME Award

Ritzy Schools received the prestigious Micro, Small, and Medium Enterprises (MSME) Award for excellence in educational innovation.
